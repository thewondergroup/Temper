02 — On the Plate

The best surf & turf brunch in London.

Lobster and steak isn't your standard bottomless brunch. Half a grilled Cornish lobster, a 28-day dry-aged British steak, a seared scallop and a chargrilled black tiger prawn — all on one plate, all cooked over open fire in our City kitchen.

It opens with freshly-shucked oysters, finishes with béarnaise and chimichurri, and runs alongside ninety minutes of free-flowing wine, beer and cocktails.

06 — FAQ

Frequently asked.

Where can I get a Lobster & Steak Bottomless Brunch in the City of London?
+
temper City serves the Lobster & Steak Bottomless Brunch every Saturday and Sunday from 11.30am, with the last seating at 3.30pm. We're at Mercers Walk in the City of London (EC2R 8AY), a five-minute walk from Bank, Moorgate and Liverpool Street stations.
What's the difference between the £70 and £80 boards?
+
The £80 board adds half a grilled Cornish lobster to your surf & turf, plus Aperol Spritz and Margarita on the cocktail list. Both boards include oysters, seared scallop, black tiger prawn, 28-day dry-aged steak, fries, house salad, béarnaise and chimichurri.
How long does bottomless last?
+
Drinks are free-flowing for 90 minutes from your booking time. The food courses are paced over the same 90 minutes so you finish with the steak still warm and your glass still full.
When is the brunch available?
+
Every Saturday and Sunday at temper City. First seating is at 11.30am and the last seating is at 3.30pm. We recommend booking in advance — Saturday lunchtime slots tend to sell out, especially in the run-up to Christmas and on bank holiday weekends.
Can I book for a large group?
+
Yes. Tables of 2–12 can book directly via the website. For groups of 12 or more, please email events@temperrestaurant.com. We regularly host birthday parties, hen dos, corporate brunches and team socials at temper City.
Does the whole table need to do bottomless?
+
Yes — bottomless is for the whole party. We're happy to cater for vegetarians, pescatarians and most allergens; please mention any dietary requirements at the time of booking so the kitchen can prepare.
What makes temper different from other bottomless brunches in London?
+
temper stands out because we cook everything over fire — giving our bottomless brunch a smoky depth most London brunches can't match. We use British 28-day dry-aged steak, Cornish lobster, hand-shucked oysters and hand-dived scallops rather than standard brunch fare. Add a central City of London location and 90 minutes of generous, unlimited drinks, and it's one of the most distinctive bottomless brunches in the capital.
