02 — Our Meat

Rare-breed beef, one farmer, in-house butchery.

We buy from one farmer — Charles Ashbridge in North Yorkshire — and we butcher and age every cut in-house. Since 2016 we've been known for rare-breed beef, bold flavours and live-fire cooking. It's all f##ing delicious.

  1. 01
    Single-farm sourcing
    One farmer in North Yorkshire. Native cross, Longhorn, Dexter — rare breeds that aren't farmed for scale.
  2. 02
    In-house butchery
    28-day minimum, 60-day average dry-aged on the bone. Cut to order.
  3. 03
    Open fire, no gas
    A custom fire pit in the middle of every room. No shortcuts.
One of London's most exciting live-fire kitchens. — Evening Standard
